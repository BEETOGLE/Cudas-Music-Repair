About Us

The owner and founder of Cuda's General Music Repair, Logan Cuda, has been operating on and repairing instruments and audio electronics for over 10 years. Over these years he has repaired and maintained dozens of instruments and audio electronics.

Cuda's General Music Repair is committed to bringing you the best service around. We are an ever-improving, evolving business here to help you and your equipment play their best.

It is our firm belief that all instruments and audio electronics deserve TLC, and Cuda's General Music Repair is here to help you give your equipment this love.
